School Motto & Crest

"Alis Propriis Vola" (Fly on your own wings)

By "Alis Propriis Vola" we mean a child of Kashmir Valley School should strive peacefully to be strong, courageous, persevering and ambitious. The Red Cross reminds us of HIM our perfect pattern of manhood who gave his life in the service of others.
The dove stands for peace and freedom and signifies the flight to freedom through the realms of knowledge, understanding and hard work.
