School Motto & Crest

"Faithful in the Least"

By our School motto " Faithful in the Least " we mean a woman who is faithful, honest, modest, generous, kind-hearted, affectionate, humble, selfless but strong and courageous in little ways that make up their span of life, for they have to prepare themselves not only for examinations but the true test of life. The crest embodies the message of enlightenment through literacy and moral character building.
