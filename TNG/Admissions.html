PARENTS ARE REQUIRED TO BRING THE FOLLOWING DOCUMENTS WITH THEM:

Photocopies of the required Documents should be serially pinned up as under:

1. One (01) Self Attested Xerox Copy of Date of Birth Certificate of the Child along with Original.
Issued by Municipal Corporation or Any other Competent Authority for Rural Area Only

     (Certificates issued by Doctor, Nursing Homes, Hospital etc will not be accepted)

2. Self Attested Xerox Copy of Photo ID Proof of Both the Parents
Aadhaar Card and Passport

3. One (01) Self Attested Xerox Copy of Hospital Discharge Certificate & Immunization Card along with Original.

4. Recent Income Certificate of Both Parents from Competent Authority
For Business Class Only Chartered Accountant’s Certificate will be accepted

5. Two(02) Colored identical copies of Recent Passport size photograph of the child. Two (02) Sets of Colored Recent Passport Size Photograph of Both Father and Mother each.
Old Photographs will not be accepted

6. In Case of Alumnus, Self Attested Provisional-cum-Character Certificate or any other School Relevant document is required.

7. In Case of a Sibling already studying in any branch school of this Society, Photocopy of latest Fee Receipt is required

8. In Case of the following Categories BPL, OBC, RBA, SC, ST or other, bring in Relevant Document.


NOTE:

  • Parents will be required to collect their wards Acknowledgment Slip from the School Authorities, the dates of interaction will be notified through SMS and will be also available on the School Notice Board/School Website (www.tbmes.org). A Registration Fee of Rs. 500/- (Non-Refundable) will have to be deposited at the time of Registration.
  • Registration does not guarantee a Call for interaction. If any Document is found to be false/incomplete or fabricated, the Registration of the Child will get cancelled without any intimation to the Parent.
  • Only those Children are Eligible for Registration whose Date of Birth falls in the Criteria decided by the School Management.
  • Enrolment for Registration does not mean that Admission is Guaranteed.
  • Only Father and/or Mother can fill up the Registration Form(s) and are required to be present on the Registration Day.
  • A Parent can apply only in one Branch of Tyndale- Biscoe & Mallinson Society. If applied in more than one Branch, the Registration will get cancelled without any intimation to the Parent.
  • Parents are requested not to bring their Child with them for the Registration Process
  • The Decision of the Admission Committee TBMS regarding Admission of the Child will be final.
  • Limited Bus Service is available for Tyndale-Biscoe & Mallinson School.
  • Bus Service is available for The Kashmir Valley School, Humhama.
